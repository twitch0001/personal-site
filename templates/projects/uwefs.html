UWE Formula Student

From 2022 to 2025 I was involved in UWE's Formula Student team, which takes part in the Formula Student Competition, a global engineering design competition, built to train engineers to improve practical skills and cohesive documentation along with competitive design.

My role went from Head of Electronics to Team Principle in my last year, where I had the fantastic opportunity to oversee the day-to-day operation of the team and ensure a smooth year. We built the team's 12th car and came a record 5th in Formula Student Spain, the team's second best result.
